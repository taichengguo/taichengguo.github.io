I am a third-year PhD student at University of Notre Dame beginning in 2023 Spring, advised by Prof. Xiangliang Zhang. Previously, I received my M.S. degree in computer science from King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). I also received my B.Eng. degree in software engineering from Xidian University.

My long-term research goal is to develop intelligent systems with Wider and Deeper reasoning abilities.
For Wider, I explore model-level scaling up (Mixture-of-experts, Multi-agents) for divergent thinking and collective intelligence and data-level scaling up (Test-Time Training)
For Deeper, I explore model-environment/tool scaling up (Multi-turn RL) for long-horizon reasoning and Thinking Paradigms scaling up (Inductive/Abductive/Deductive).
For practical applications, my studies encompass LLM for Data Science (code generation, hyperparameter optimization, etc), Recommender Systems and Scientific domains.
